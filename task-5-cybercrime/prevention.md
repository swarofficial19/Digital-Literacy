Part B: Prevention Checklist 
'Stay Safe Online' Guide for Students


UPI PIN Rule: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; a PIN is only required to send money.


QR Code Caution: Be wary of QR codes sent over chat apps; legitimate platforms do not require scanning a code to pay you.


Enable 2FA: Always use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for your email and financial accounts.


Strong Passwords: Use unique, complex passwords for every account and change them every 90 days.


Verify Links: Hover over links in emails to check the actual URL before clicking to avoid phishing sites.


Public Wi-Fi: Avoid logging into bank accounts or making payments while connected to public or unsecured Wi-Fi networks.


Official Apps: Only download banking or payment apps from the official Google Play Store or Apple App Store.


Check Permissions: Periodically review app permissions and revoke access to your contacts or camera for apps that don't need them.
