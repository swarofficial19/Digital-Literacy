For my professional digital portfolio, I established a presence on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Stack Overflow. I chose GitHub because it is the industry standard for version control and showcasing technical projects through code repositories. LinkedIn was selected to build a professional network, connect with recruiters, and document my academic journey at VIT Bhopal. Lastly, I chose Stack Overflow to engage with the global developer community, learn from expert solutions, and eventually contribute back by answering technical queries.Over the next four years of my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering, I plan to use these platforms as a "living resume." I will use GitHub to host my course assignments and personal projects to show my growth in languages like Java. On LinkedIn, I will share my certifications and internship experiences to stay visible to potential employers. Stack Overflow will be my primary resource for troubleshooting complex coding issues and staying updated on software trends. Together, these platforms will help me transition from a student to a professional engineer with a verifiable track record.
